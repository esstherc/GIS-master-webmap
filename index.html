About This Project

Project Overview:

This interactive map displays GIS Master's programs across the United States, providing comprehensive and up-to-date information (as of September 2025) about 172 programs at 140 institutions. Each program is categorized by its primary focus—Professional, Research, or Mixed—to help prospective students, educators, and researchers explore GIS graduate education across the U.S.

This visualization is one of the map products developed for my M.S. thesis titled “GIScience Education from Faculty Careers to Student Training: A Dual Perspective on Academic and Professional Development.” The project aims to analyze trends in GIS education by synthesizing faculty hiring data, curriculum structures, and program-level characteristics across academic and professional tracks.

The full thesis is available through the University of Wisconsin–Madison’s institutional repository: https://minds.wisconsin.edu/handle/1793/95176

Data Sources:

• Program information manually collected from university websites

Features:

• Interactive filtering by state, program type, and format
• Detailed program information in popups
• Save programs to wishlist or add notes
• Export saved programs to CSV
